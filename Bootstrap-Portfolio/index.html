About Me

My name is Joe Thor. I was born in Dubuque, Iowa. I graduated Luther College. I received my undergraduate degree in history and classics. For my classics degree I studied ancient Greek and Latin along with ancient mediterranean cultures. In my senior year, I worked closely with Dr. Dan Davis to developed a field guide on ancient amphorae. Amphorae are large ceramic jugs which were used to carry dry and wet goods such as grain, wine, olive oil, and fish sauce. They were ubiqitous in the ancient world and were unique enough in their dimensions and the make up of the ceramic to determine from where they originated. My work with Dr. Davis earned me an invitation to work aboard the Exploration Vessel Nautilus where we used sonar and remotely operated vehicles to explore ancient shipwrecks in the Black Sea.
