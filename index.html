About Me

I am a web developer with a background in Javascript and Python. I love adapting technologies to solve interesting issues. Currently, I am employed at the University of Minnesota as a business and systems analyst supporting the Oracle financial database.

I attended Luther College in Decorah, Iowa as an undergraduate and received my BA in ancient languages and history. At Luther, I learned the art of learning, which I have leveraged to teach myself HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, and SQL. I love the challenge of a new language, be it ancient or computer based.

In the summer of 2018, I enrolled in the University of Minnesota's Coding Boot Camp and have enjoyed the challenges of weekly challenges and coding in groups.
